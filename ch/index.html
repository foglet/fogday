要不要 (yào bù yào)

A guide to using "要不要" in Mandarin conversations

1. Asking if someone wants something

Example: 你要不要喝茶？

Zhuyin: ㄋㄧˇ ㄧㄠˋ ㄅㄨˋ ㄧㄠˋ ㄏㄜ ㄔㄚˊ

Pinyin: Nǐ yào bù yào hē chá?

Translation: Do you want to drink tea?

2. Offering to do something together

Example: 我們要不要去看電影？

Zhuyin: ㄨㄛˇ ㄇㄣ˙ ㄧㄠˋ ㄅㄨˋ ㄧㄠˋ ㄑㄩˋ ㄎㄢˋ ㄉㄧㄢˋ ㄧㄥˇ

Pinyin: Wǒmen yào bù yào qù kàn diànyǐng?

Translation: Do we want to go see a movie?

3. Suggesting a choice

Example: 你要不要先做完功課？

Zhuyin: ㄋㄧˇ ㄧㄠˋ ㄅㄨˋ ㄧㄠˋ ㄒㄧㄢ ㄗㄨㄛˋ ㄨㄢˊ ㄍㄨㄥ ㄎㄜˋ

Pinyin: Nǐ yào bù yào xiān zuò wán gōngkè?

Translation: Do you want to finish your homework first?

4. Checking if someone wants to join

Example: 你要不要跟我們去吃飯？

Zhuyin: ㄋㄧˇ ㄧㄠˋ ㄅㄨˋ ㄧㄠˋ ㄍㄣ ㄨㄛˇ ㄇㄣ˙ ㄑㄩˋ ㄔ ㄈㄢˋ

Pinyin: Nǐ yào bù yào gēn wǒmen qù chī fàn?

Translation: Do you want to come eat with us?

5. Offering help

Example: 你要不要我幫忙？

Zhuyin: ㄋㄧˇ ㄧㄠˋ ㄅㄨˋ ㄧㄠˋ ㄨㄛˇ ㄅㄤ ㄇㄤˊ

Pinyin: Nǐ yào bù yào wǒ bāngmáng?

Translation: Do you want me to help?
